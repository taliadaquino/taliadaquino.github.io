That large square ungainly ARCHITECT

with a hole in the centre is no more.

Whimpering Sfb filing clerks will be

lulled as the new form A4 ARCHITECT,

the first seen since February 1969,

springs into view. The squeeze in

credit has squeezed our square format

out of existance. Readers will be

saddened by the passing of the hole.  

To help them survive this calamitous

loss, and for this issue only, at no

extra charge to regular subscribers,

this do-it-yourself hole is provided.
