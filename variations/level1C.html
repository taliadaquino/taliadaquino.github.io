There

Not

Accept
that

draft.
It

to
get
done.


There

no
editing
stage.


Pretending
you
know
what

doing
is
almost
the
same
as
knowing
what
you are
doing,
so
just
accept
that
you
know&
what you’re
doing
even
if
you
don’t
and
do
it.


Banish
procrastination.
If
you
wait
more
than
a
week
to
get
an
idea
done,
abandon
it.



The
point
of
being
done
is
not
to
finish
but
to
get
other
things
done.



Once
you’re
done
you
can
throw
it
away.



Laugh
at
perfection.
It’s
boring
and
keeps
you
from
being
done.



People

dirty
hands
are
wrong.
Doing
something
makes
you
right.



Failure

done.
So

Destruction
is
a
variant

done.



If
you
have
an
idea
and
publish
it
on
the

internet,
that
counts
as
a
ghost
of
done.



Done
is
the
engine

more.
